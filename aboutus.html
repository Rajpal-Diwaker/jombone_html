ABOUT JOMBONE

In 2016, Jombone was born with a mission to eliminate costly inefficiencies in the temporary work hiring industry. Our goal is to create a transparent end to end hiring, staffing and employment management framework where companies and workers share an employment ready relationship; conveniently accessed using our mobile apps and website.


To increase the ROI for the dollars spent by eliminating ‘middle-man’ costs; hiring motivated, quality workers who become empowered, engaged team members.
To improve and enrich the lives of millions of families who are dependent on industrial-type work with limited specialized skills and the strong desire to better themselves as they build their skill-sets.

Mission Statement

For Companies:

To increase the ROI for the dollars spent by eliminating ‘middle-man’ costs; hiring
motivated, quality workers who become empowered, engaged team
members.

For Workers:

To improve and enrich the lives of millions of families who are dependent on
industrial-type work with limited specialized skills and the strong desire to
better themselves as they build their skill-sets.

At Jombone, we believe companies need a simpler way to hire and manage
employments. That’s why we pioneered the industrial staffing market, and
it’s why we are committed to continuous innovation.
Our Vision
  • To build trust and provide a truly ‘world-class’ product and service to our clients
  • Bringing together over 100s of millions of workers and companies
  • Become the employment management platform of choice for companies to hire temporary workers
  • Provide continuous skills enhancement programs and training opportunities offering ongoing career growth to those in need!
Our Values
  • We do it by staying focused on our core values:
  • We always act with Integrity
  • We are honest and transparent
  • We are committed to our communities
  • We always act in the best interest of our customers
